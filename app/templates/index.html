FAQ

What is a Persona Cartridge?

A portable, model-independent package containing identity, communication style, values, goals, boundaries, preferences, and behavioral rules.

Can I edit it later?

Yes. Every cartridge can be revisited and refined.

Does it work with multiple AI models?

Yes. Persona Cartridges are model-independent — use them with Gemini, Claude, GPT, Mistral, Qwen, and more.

Can I import my existing characters?

Absolutely. Feed CHIMERA RP sheets, stories, bios, lore, dialogue, prompt files, or notes. It discovers what's missing.

Does CHIMERA replace my AI model?

No. CHIMERA creates the personality. You still need a runtime — that's what ARCHE provides.

What happens after I create one?

You get a Persona Cartridge you can load into ARCHE and use immediately with any supported model.
